BBQ PRAWNS

INGREDIENTS:

  • - 1‚ÅÑ4 cup coarsely chopped coriander
  • - 1/4 cup coarsely chopped mint
  • - 2 tbs coarsely chopped flat-leaf parsley
  • - 2 tbs chopped oregano
  • - 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • - 1/4 cup (60ml) white wine vinegar
  • - 1/4 cup (60ml) extra virgin olive oil
  • - 1.5kg Raw King Prawns, peeled leaving tails intact, deveined

STEPS:

Combine the coriander, mint, parsley, oregano, garlic, vinegar and oil in a medium bowl. Season. Place the prawns in a large bowl. Add 2 tbs of the coriander mixture and toss to combine. Heat a barbecue grill or chargrill on medium-high. Cook the prawns, in batches, turning occasionally, for 5 mins or until prawns curl and change colour. Transfer to a serving platter. Drizzle with the remaining coriander mixture to serve.

IDEAL FOR:

Diet, healthy, bulking

NUTRITION:

-Calories:134 calories -Protein:24 g -Fat:4g -Carbs:3g
