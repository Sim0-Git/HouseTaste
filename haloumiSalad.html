HALOUMI SALAD

INGREDIENTS:

  • - 250g pkt pearl couscous
  • - 2 x 250g pkts haloumi sliced
  • - 1 tablespoon Moroccan spice mix
  • - 2 ​zucchini​, sliced
  • - 250g cherry tomato medley, halved
  • - 1/2 cup fresh continental parsley leaves
  • - 2 tablespoons Greek yoghurt
  • - 1 small garlic clove, crushed
  • - 1 lemon, rind finely grated, juiced
  • - 2 wholemeal pita breads, toasted

STEPS:

Cook couscous in a saucepan of boiling water following packet directions. Drain. Meanwhile, pat haloumi dry with paper towel. Sprinkle with spice mix. Spray a non-stick frying pan with oil. Heat pan over medium heat. Cook haloumi 1-2 minutes each side or until golden. Cook zucchini 1-2 minutes until just coloured. Combine couscous, zucchini, tomatoes, parsley and mint in a large bowl. Season well. Combine tahini, yoghurt, garlic and lemon. Add lemon juice to taste. Divide salad among serving bowls or arrange on a platter. Top with haloumi and pomegranate. Drizzle with dressing. Serve with pita.

IDEAL FOR:

Diet, healthy, bulking

NUTRITION:

-Calories:134 calories -Protein:7.3g -Fat:10.4g -Carbs:3g
